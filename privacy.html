Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Overview

MacBuddy does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device. Screenshots, clipboard history, and other feature data stay local and are never uploaded.

Data We Do Not Collect

MacBuddy does not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, device identifiers, or any other personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, and no tracking of any kind.

In-App Purchases

In-app purchases (tip jar) are processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We receive only a purchase confirmation — no payment details, billing address, or financial information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for how they handle payments.

Third-Party Services

MacBuddy does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

Translation Feature

The screenshot translation feature uses Apple's built-in translation framework. Text processing happens on-device or through Apple's services, subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. MacBuddy does not send translation data to any other third party.

Children's Privacy

MacBuddy does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at dev@atlai.co.uk.
